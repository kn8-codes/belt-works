# Belt.works Copy Rules

Updated: 2026-05-01

## Core lines

- We build software that works. You bring the problem.
- Research first. Software as evidence.

## Tone

Plain. Useful. Direct. Built for people who need the thing to work.

## Do

- Describe the transaction clearly.
- Point to proof.
- Keep service language concrete.
- Route serious problems to `/contact`.

## Do not

- Use startup theater.
- Use agency boilerplate.
- Use fake scarcity or fake community language.
- Lead with local-underdog framing.
- Mention retired intake labels or stale client-demo material.
